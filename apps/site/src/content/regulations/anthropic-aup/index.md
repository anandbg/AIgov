Anthropic's Acceptable Use Policy is the binding statement of what Claude — and any Anthropic-served model — can and cannot lawfully be used for. It is the document an enterprise legal team will reach for when scoping a Claude-backed workload.

Tracked **daily** because Anthropic has historically amended the AUP with short notice, especially around expanded categories of prohibited use.
