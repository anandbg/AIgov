Acme Robotics is based in Ohio, US, with floor-tested deployments in 14 distribution centres across North America. The protagonist, Maya Chen, joined as VP Engineering eighteen months ago and inherited an AI deployment landscape with two production systems, no policy, and one near-miss safety incident.

Acme is the recurring US-jurisdiction case study for this site. Their journey through the 12 stages emphasises voluntary-framework adoption (NIST AI RMF) and pre-emptive risk-tier rigor in a market that does not have an EU-style horizontal AI law.
